Our Story

Crafted with passion,
served with love

Kopi Kesidang was born from a simple idea — that great coffee deserves a great experience. Named after the delicate Kesidang flower, we bring together the finest beans, locally inspired flavours, and a cosy atmosphere that feels like home.

Every item on our menu is prepared fresh, from our signature bear-shaped lattes to our flaky hand-rolled croissants.
